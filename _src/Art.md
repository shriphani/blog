# ART

<u>Synesthesia - For The Love Of Music</u><br>
<span style="text-align:right; font-size:15px;">- Shriphani Palakodety, Pem Lasota, Aram Ebtekar</span>

Synesthesia explores interior design that emphasizes the music experience. Music shouldn't just be an aural experience. It should be a visual treat.

<img src="/img/matrix.jpg" width="80%" /><br />

The first of many devices is a picture frame dedicated to music response. 4096 LEDs listen via a microphone and produce a beautiful experience.

This project was built in collaboration with two of the most talented people I know - [Pem Lasota](https://interactive.mit.edu/about/people/pem) and [Aram Ebtekar](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~aebtekar/). [Parthasarathy Trivedi](http://www.ptrivedi.com/) helped us 3D print a case.
